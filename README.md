A crypto scanner that monitors the top 100 tokens by market cap and hunts for two specific setups across three timeframes (4H, 1H, 30M). The first is the 3-Drive harmonic pattern confirmed by RSI divergence, price printing three higher highs while RSI prints three lower highs(bearish), or the inverse for bullish. Signals get a confidence score built from divergence strength plus time symmetry between the three drives. The second is a Bollinger Band squeeze: bands physically converging for at least 4 consecutive candles while width is below its recent average, which flags volatility compression before a potential breakout. Data comes from Coinbase with Binance as fallback, rankings from CoinGecko. It auto-rescans on a timer, so you can leave it running on a second monitor and check back when something fires.
